## What Happened

The US-Iran conflict escalated sharply on Friday when Iran shot down two US military aircraft — an F-15E Strike Eagle and reportedly an A-10 attack aircraft. One American crew member was rescued; a second remains missing in a mountainous region of southwestern Iran. President Trump responded by giving Iran 48 hours to reopen the Strait of Hormuz, the world's most critical oil shipping lane, or face intensified strikes.

The war, now in its sixth week since joint US-Israeli strikes began on February 28, has killed thousands, disrupted global shipping, and spiked fuel prices worldwide.
